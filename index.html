Our Philosophy

Healing Rooted in
Timeless Tradition

Arya Vaidya Sala, founded in 1902 by the legendary Vaidyaratnam P.S. Varier in Kottakkal, Kerala, is one of the world's foremost institutions of Ayurveda. Our Ponda branch carries forward this sacred legacy — offering classical Ayurvedic medicines of the highest quality to the people of Goa.

Every medicine here is prepared with time-honoured recipes, using pure herbs harvested at the right seasons, processed with traditional methods in GMP-certified facilities. No shortcuts. No compromises.

Kerala · Goa · Ayurveda

Where Two Coasts
Share One Healing Art

Kerala's lush Western Ghats have gifted the world Ayurveda's finest herbs — turmeric, ginger, pepper, holy basil, brahmi and countless others. Goa's tropical climate, with its red laterite soil and coastal abundance, creates the perfect sanctuary to receive these ancient medicines.

At Kottakkal Ponda, we honour both lands — bringing Kerala's most trusted Ayurvedic brand to Goa's vibrant heart, serving families across the Konkan coast.
