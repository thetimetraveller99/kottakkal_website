A pioneering institution that has kept the lamp of Ayurveda burning for over 120 years.

Our Heritage

The Legacy of
Vaidyaratnam P.S. Varier

In 1902, Vaidyaratnam Pannikar Sankaran Varier founded the Arya Vaidya Sala in the small town of Kottakkal in Kerala's Malappuram district, with a singular vision: to revive and preserve the ancient science of Ayurveda for the world.

What began as a modest clinic and pharmacy has grown into one of the world's most respected Ayurvedic institutions — manufacturing over 530 classical formulations, operating treatment centres across India and internationally, and conducting research that bridges ancient wisdom with modern science.

The Ponda branch carries this same spirit to Goa — providing access to authentic Kottakkal medicines under qualified medical supervision, serving the families of the Konkan coast with the same integrity and quality the institution has maintained for over a century.

Our Story
1902

Founded in Kottakkal

Vaidyaratnam P.S. Varier establishes Arya Vaidya Sala with a small dispensary and medicine manufacturing unit in Kottakkal, Kerala.

1920s–40s

Growth & Expansion

AVS expands its classical formulation library, reviving rare Ashtanga Hridayam and Charaka Samhita prescriptions. The institution becomes a beacon of authentic Ayurveda.

1970s

Research & Education

Formal research wing established. AVS begins training Ayurvedic physicians and publishing classical texts and commentaries.

1990s

GMP Certification & Modernisation

Quality control laboratory earns AYUSH accreditation. Manufacturing processes are modernised while preserving traditional methods.

2000s

International Recognition

AVS products are exported worldwide. Treatment centres open in multiple countries, bringing Kottakkal's healing tradition to a global audience.

Present

Kottakkal Ponda, Goa

Serving the people of Goa and the Konkan coast with authentic Kottakkal medicines under qualified Ayurvedic consultation.

What Makes Kottakkal Special

Unlike mass-produced supplements, every Kottakkal medicine follows the exact classical recipe from ancient Sanskrit texts — the Ashtanga Hridayam, Charaka Samhita, and Sushruta Samhita. Herbs are sourced from authenticated regions, dried and processed at precise temperatures, and combined in the ratios the texts prescribe.

The result is medicine that works — not just because of individual ingredients, but because of the synergy created by centuries of tested formulation science.
